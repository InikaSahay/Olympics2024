Featured Athletes

Neeraj Chopra

Subedar Major Neeraj Chopra PVSM VSM is an Indian track and field athlete. Considered as the greatest Indian javelin thrower, he's a double medalist at the Olympics, World Championships, and the Asian Games. He has won the Diamond League once.

Simone Biles

SSimone Arianne Biles Owens OLY is an American artistic gymnast. Her 11 Olympic medals and 30 World Championship medals make her the most decorated gymnast in history, and she is widely considered to be the greatest gymnast of all time.

Parul Chaudhary

Indian athlete Parul Chaudhary bagged a spot at the Paris Olympics when she finished 11th in the final of the 3000m steeplechase event in Budapest last year.
